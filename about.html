About

NIL has no founder to introduce. There is no story of a person who started this, no portrait, no path from burnout to clarity.

The brand is a position, not a personality. The position is this: you were already enough before any product, any score, any brand arrived. It can be held by anyone who shares it.

There is nothing here to follow. There is no one here to become.
